Questions

Things parents ask us.

Is this safe for my kid?
Yes — and we take that seriously. Every input from your child is filtered before reaching the AI. Every response is governed by Koda's coaching values. There are no ads, no data sales, and you can read every conversation. We built this for our own kids first.
What ages is this for?
WildCoders is designed for kids ages 8 to 13. Koda meets them where they are — whether they're brand new to coding or already building. The platform stays one step ahead, growing with your child.
How is Koda different from ChatGPT?
ChatGPT gives kids answers. Koda asks them questions. ChatGPT was built for adults; Koda was built for kids ages 8-13. ChatGPT has no values; Koda is governed by four coaching principles that mirror what great human teachers do. The difference is the difference between dependency and capability.
Can Koda help with homework and schoolwork?
Yes — Koda is built to support classroom learning, homework, and school projects. We work directly with school districts to integrate her into existing tech and lesson plans, with live progress dashboards for teachers. If you'd like to bring WildCoders to your school, reach out at hello@wildcoders.org.
Does my kid need to know how to code?
No. Koda guides them through everything. Most kids start by describing what they want to build in plain English, and Koda walks them through the thinking. Real coding concepts come later — once curiosity is already lit.
Can I see what my kid is doing?
Yes. After every Build Mode session, you get a parent report showing what your child worked on, the questions Koda asked, and where they're heading next. Full transparency — that's the deal.
How does the $1-to-schools program actually work?
When you sign up, you pick the school your kid attends. From that point on, $1 of every monthly subscription is set aside for that school and sent quarterly. No extra cost to you, no opt-in required, no fine print. If your kid changes schools, you can update it anytime in your account.
What if my kid doesn't like it?
Cancel anytime within the 7-day trial and you won't be charged. After that, cancel anytime — no contracts, no hassle.
