What the first session looks like
1
Koda asks your child 5 quick design questions — topic, quiz length, difficulty, favorite animal, and quiz name
2
Koda builds a real, playable quiz from their answers — usually in under 30 seconds
3
Your child plays their quiz, then tells Koda what to change — harder, different animals, more rounds
4
Koda rebuilds it. They iterate. The whole first session takes about 15–25 minutes
💡 Tip for parents: Sit with them for the first session. You'll be amazed at the decisions they make when they're the one in charge.
Koda's rules — what parents need to know
1.Koda only talks about building. If a child asks about anything else, Koda redirects to the project.
2.Koda never does the work. He guides, questions, and builds what the child describes. The thinking is always theirs.
3.Koda never asks for personal information. No names beyond what you gave us. No school, address, or contact details.
4.Every response is filtered for age-appropriate content. Two layers of safety checks before anything reaches your child.
5.Koda is always kind. He challenges kids to think more carefully, but he never criticises, dismisses, or discourages.
